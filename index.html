Why do people love cats?

There are various reasons why people love cats. Some of the common reasons are:

  1. Cats are gorgeous felines and have an incredibly elegant and unique appearance
  2. Cats are independent, making them low-maintenance pets for busy people.
  3. Kittens are absolutely adorable. Their very existence can melt a cold person's heart
  4. Cats are naturally playful, curious, and energetic. Simply watching and observing them will bring you joy.
  5. A cat's very presence can help calm someone down and reduce their risk of heart attacks

Introducing five (5) cat websites you can visit to either better your day or learn something new!:

1. LoveMeow

 Love Meow is the perfect site to view stories, pictures and videos of kittens.
 You'll get beautiful stories of kittens finding homes of their own to videos of kittens being simply adorable.

2. moderncat

 moderncat is a website that provides a lot of information, resources and tips regarding cats to all cat lovers/owners.
 The website delves into the behaviour of cats, resolutions to confusing cat issues, best cat products, essential tips for cat care, etc.
 moderncat also publishes magazines featuring articles on cat breeds, cat care, behaviour, nourishment, and much more.

3. TheCatSite

 As its name suggests, TheCatSite is a site that is all about cats.
 Here you will find forums or communities of dedicated cat lovers sharing their experiences with cats and their love for cats.
 You will receive advice on the behaviour and personality of cats, cat care, cat health, and so much more!

4. PURRsonals

 PURRsonals is a site that helps to bring like-minded cat people together.
 This is a space where individuals can share with others, their love for cats as well as their stories, hobbies, and feelings of or with their cats.

5. RSPCA

 If you're a cat lover/owner and want to learn more about how to properly care for cats, then RSPCA is the purrfect site for you.
 From grooming to feeding to veterinery care, RSPCA will teach you all that a cat needs to stay clean, fit, and healthy.
